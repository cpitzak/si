Facebook (FB) Fundamental Analysis - Depreciation / Gross Profit

Overview

We are going to analyze Facebook (FB) financial statements using the methods described in Warren Buffett and the Interpretation of Financial Statements.

The goal will be to see if Facebook (FB) has a durable competitive advantage according to the methods described in the book. We will use historical data from 2010 to 2018.

The financial statements can be found at EDGAR Company Filings. On EDGAR in the Fast Search enter the ticker: FB

The Income Statement, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow data is in the 10-K filings.

Analyzing Depreciation / Gross Profit

What Warren has discovered is that companies that have a durable competitive advantage tend to have lower depreciation costs as a percentage of gross profit than companies that have to suffer the woes of intense competition. page 48 Warren Buffett and the Interpretation of Financial Statements Published: Jan 2011.
