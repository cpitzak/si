Analyzing Selling, General & Administrative / Gross Profit

In the search for a company with a durable competitive advantage the lower the company's SGA expenses, the better. If they can stay consistently low, all the better. In the world of business anything under 30% is fantastic. However, there are a number of companies with a durable competitive advantage that have SGA expenses in the 30% to 80% range. page 39-40 Warren Buffett and the Interpretation of Financial Statements Published: Jan 2011.
