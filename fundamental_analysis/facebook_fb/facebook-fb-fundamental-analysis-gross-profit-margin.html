Analyzing Gross Profit Margin

As a very general rule (and there are exceptions): Companies with gross profit margins of 40% or better thend to be companies with some sort of durable competitive advantage. page 34 Warren Buffett and the Interpretation of Financial Statements Published: Jan 2011.

Gross Profit Margin = (Revenue - Cost of Goods Sold) / Revenue
