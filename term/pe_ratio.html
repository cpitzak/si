PE Ratios

There are multiple kinds of PE Ratios. The most popular are:

  • Trailing Twelve Month PE Ratio (TTM)
  • Forward PE Ratio
  • PE Ratio without non-recurring items (NRI)
  • Shiller PE Ratio

PE Ratio (TTM)

The Trailing Twelve Month PE Ratio (TTM) is calculated as

PE Ratio = Share Price / Earnings per Share (Diluted) (TTM)

PE Ratio Calculated for Whole Company

It can also be calcuated for the whole company:

PE Ratio = Market Cap / Net Income

Forward PE Ratio

Forward PE Ratio is calculated by using the expected earnings for the next twelve months or the next full-year fiscal period.
