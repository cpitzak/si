Market Capitalization

Market Capitalization is the total dollar market value of a company's outstanding shares of stock. It is the total market value ot buy the whole company.

Market Cap = Current Share Price * Total Number of Shares Outstanding

The market capitalization allows investors to understand the size of a company. There are three market value ranges that are commonly used among investors to refer to the size of a company.

  • Large Cap - Company's with a market value of $10 billion or more.
  • Mid-Cap - Company's with a market value between $2 billion and $10 billion.
  • Small-Cap - Company's with a market value between $300 million to $2 billion.
