Price-to-Sales Ratio - P/S Ratio

The P/S ratio is used to value a company's stock price to its revenues. The P/S ratio shows how much an investor is willing to pay for each dollar of sales. The calculation is

PS Ratio = Share Price / Revenue per Share (TTM)

The P/S ratio can also be calcuated for the whole company as

PS Ratio = Market Cap / Revenue

The P/S ratio is best used to value cyclical businesses instead of the P/E ratio because the P/E ratio peforms poorly for cyclical businesses.

Warren Buffett estimates the broad market value and projected future returns of the stock market by doing a P/S ratio calculation on the whole stock market. The P/S Ratio for the whole stock market is calculated as

PS Ratio = Total Market Cap of all Stocks / GDP of the Country
